    📖 How to Play Emotional Chess

    🎯 Basic Chess Rules
    • Each player takes turns moving one piece
    • Capture opponent pieces by moving to their square
    • Protect your King from check and checkmate
    • Win by checkmating the opponent's King
    ❤️ Love Mechanics
    • Formation: Pieces within 3 squares can fall in love
    • Protection: Lovers cannot capture each other
    • Defense: Lovers ignore each other's attack zones
    • Ending: Love ends if distance > 3 or either piece is in check
    😡 Anger Mechanics
    • Trigger: When your piece is threatened or captured
    • Effect: All nearby allies get +1 movement range
    • Duration: Anger lasts for 1 turn only
    • Exception: Knights are not affected by anger
    😢 Sadness Mechanics
    • Trigger: When your lover is captured
    • Effect: You cannot move for 1 turn
    • Exception: Kings cannot become sad
    • Emergency: Can move only to resolve check
    🎮 Multiplayer Features
    • Turn-based: Only current player can move
    • Real-time: All players see moves instantly
    • Emotions: Live emotion tracking during gameplay
    • Chat: Game log shows all moves and events
    💡 Strategy Tips
    • Use love to protect important pieces
    • Trigger anger strategically for powerful moves
    • Be careful not to lose your lover to sadness
    • Watch the emotion display for tactical advantages
